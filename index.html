About Us

Aswar Al Khaleej International Est. General Contracting, headquartered in Khobar, Saudi Arabia, has been a pillar of excellence in the general contracting and manpower industry since 2011. As a privately held, Saudi-owned firm, we are proud to be recognized as an "EXCELLENT" grade establishment, setting high standards in the contracting sector across the Kingdom.

Our company thrives on the visionary leadership of industry experts with over a decade of experience. With a commitment to innovation, Aswar Al Khaleej has earned a reputation for delivering exceptional results by prioritizing client satisfaction and fostering long-term relationships.
