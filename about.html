About Us

Who We Are

Aswar Al Khaleej International Est. General Contracting, headquartered in Khobar, Saudi Arabia, has been a pillar of excellence in the general contracting and manpower industry since 2011. As a privately held, Saudi-owned firm, we are proud to be recognized as an "EXCELLENT" grade establishment, setting high standards in the contracting sector across the Kingdom.

Our company thrives on the visionary leadership of industry experts with over a decade of experience. With a commitment to innovation, Aswar Al Khaleej has earned a reputation for delivering exceptional results by prioritizing client satisfaction and fostering long-term relationships. More than 60% of our business comes from repeat clients, a testament to the trust we have built over the years.

At Aswar Al Khaleej, our teams are client-centric, tailored to understand and meet the unique demands of every project. We pride ourselves on the integrity, expertise, and dedication of our people, continuously evolving to meet the ever-changing needs of the industry. Our foundation is built on strong values, technical knowledge, and unwavering commitment, ensuring that we deliver quality and excellence in every endeavor.

To be the leading general contracting and manpower solutions provider in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, recognized for our unwavering commitment to excellence, innovation, and client satisfaction. We aim to set new industry benchmarks by consistently delivering high-quality projects and services that not only meet but exceed the expectations of our clients, ostering long-term partnerships built on trust, integrity, and expertise. Our vision is to contribute to the growth and development of Saudi Arabia’s infrastructure while maintaining our position as a trusted, reliable, and innovative market leader.

Our mission is to deliver superior contracting and manpower solutions through a client-focused approach, ensuring the highest standards of quality, safety, and efficiency in every project. We are committed to building lasting relationships with our clients by consistently providing innovative, timely, and cost-effective services. Through the dedication of our skilled teams, we aim to contribute to the growth of the Kingdom’s infrastructure while upholding our core values of integrity, professionalism, and excellence. We strive to create value for our clients, employees, and the communities we serve.

HEALTH, SAFETY, AND ENVIRONMENTAL (HSE) POLICY

At Aswar Al Khaleej International Est. General Contracting, we prioritize the health, safety, and well-being of our employees, clients, and communities. We are committed to:

  • Ensuring a safe work environment with zero accidents through strict safety protocols and continuous training.
  • Complying with all health, safety, and environmental regulations in Saudi Arabia
  • Reducing environmental impact through responsible waste management, resource efficiency, and sustainable practices.
  • Fostering a culture of accountability, where every employee contributes to HSE excellence
  • Continuously improving our HSE practices to exceed industry standards

Our goal is to protect people and the planet while delivering exceptional results.

QUALITY POLICY

At Aswar Al Khaleej International Est. General Contracting, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional quality in all our projects and services. Our commitment includes:
